Organization type definitions

  • A Ontario government name includes higher levels of government. For example, a ministry or department.
  • B Ontario government name includes other government entities. For example, a commission, inquiry, board, bureau, or jail.
  • C Ontario government name includes lower levels of government. For example, a division, branch, or program.
  • Corporate name includes names of businesses and corporations.
  • Geographic name includes names of cities, towns, counties, or districts.
