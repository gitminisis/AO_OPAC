History Card Registration

If you want to see original documents at London Metropolitan Archives you need to register for a free City of London History Card. Follow the link below to begin the online registration process. We recommend registering before you visit, but you can also complete the registration form when you arrive. When you arrive in our Information Area, our team will take your photograph and provide your new City of London History Card.

While it is possible to visit us without a History Card (whether you want to use our London history library, watch films or browse the printed maps in our mediatheque or visit our exhibition), we highly recommend obtaining one. Nothing quite beats the experience of using original documents for the first time!

Who can register for a History Card?

Anyone over the age of 14 can apply for a History Card.

Why do I need one?

Everyone needs a History Card to gain access to original documents at London Metropolitan Archives. The History Card will also enable you to carry out your research more effectively by providing access to enhanced features in this catalogue. You'll be able to request original documents to view them in the Archives Study Area, make Bookmarks (create a list of documents in the catalogue for future reference) and see a list of documents that you've previously requested during an earlier visit. You can also request documents up to two weeks in advance, reserving them for your visit and ensuring that you can make the most of your research time.

How do I get one?

You can complete the registration form using the computers at London Metropolitan Archives or start your registration up to a month before your visit to London Metropolitan Archives by clicking the button below.

Your details will be held on our system for 30 days before being deleted. During this time you can complete your registration process by bringing two proof of identity documents (one proof of identity and one proof of address) to the Information Area at London Metropolitan Archives where we will validate your application and take a photograph of you for the card and our record.

What documents will I need to register?

Acceptable documents for registration are:

Proof of your identity (must include a valid signature):

  • Passport
  • Driving Licence
  • Bank Card
  • Credit Card
  • National ID card
  • Police/Customs/Home Office/warrant card
  • Forces ID card
  • Sea/Air Masters licence
  • NUJ Pass
  • Government/local council staff ID

Proof of your address (internet printouts or online statements are not accepted):

  • Utility bill (issued within the last three months)
  • Bank/building society statement (issued within the last three months)
  • Credit card statement (issued within the last three months)
  • Council tax bill
  • HM Revenue and Customs Statement
  • Passports with address (where the address has been officially entered by the issuing authority)
  • National ID card with address
  • Driving licence with address
  • Home Office permit to stay
  • University certificate for hall of residence (stamped and signed by institution)
  • TV Licence
  • Firearms licence
  • Home Insurance policy
  • International student photocard

Proof for students

If you are aged 14 or over and still at school or college you should bring a letter on headed notepaper, signed by your teacher or tutor, together with some evidence of your age, such as a passport or birth certificate.
