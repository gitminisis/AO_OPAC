Thank You for Registering

Your details have been registered successfully !

If you are already at the London Metropolitan Archives, please go to the Information Point, where one of our staff will check your registration details and your proof of identity, and will issue you with your History card.

If you have registered from your computer at home or work, please go to the Information Point when you arrive on your next visit to the London Metropolitan Archives. Please ensure that you bring with you the proof of identity which you specified while registering.

Please note that your details will be held for 30 days. If you do not visit the London Metropolitan Archives within this time to complete your registration, your details will be deleted.
